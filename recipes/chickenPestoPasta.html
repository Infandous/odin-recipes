Chicken Pesto Pasta

This Pasta is a great and relatively healthy meal especially after Workouts!

This recipe is incredibly simple to make and a great
addition to your reperotoire! The first dish I ever created
it is one of my personal favorites.

Ingredients

  • 3 chicken breasts
  • jar of pesto
  • angel hair pasta
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • olive oil
  • Italian seasoning

Steps

  1. Heat up olive oil in pan while cutting up chicken into bite size pieces
  2. cook chicken on medium heat
  3. turn chicken every few minutes, for first 2-3 turns add garlic, onion, and italian seasoning
  4. Cook pasta to al dente, while doing so cook chicken on high turning every minute
  5. While pasta is cooking, heat olive oil on low heat
  6. Mix and enjoy!
