Stir Fry

Ever since I started learning to speak Mandarin
I gained a love for Chinese and other Asian cuisine.
I cannot confirm the authenticity of this dish given
I came up with it myself, but it sure tastes good!

Ingredients

  • Rice Vinegar
  • Sesame Oil
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sischuan peppers
  • Siracha
  • noodles
  • beef
  • stir fry veggies

Steps

  1. head up cast iron with rice vinegar, oil, and soy sauce
  2. cook beef in cast iron until almost cooked
  3. mix in veggies and pour water into pan
  4. grind up Sischuan peppers and mix in
  5. Pour siracha into pan
  6. cook until water is almost all evaporated
  7. Serve and enjoy!
