Chciken Parmesan

My Uncle's chicken parmesan recipe. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • grape seed oil
  • Flour
  • 2-3 eggs
  • milk
  • bread crumbs
  • angel hair pasta
  • alfredo sauce

Steps

  1. Pound chicken breasts
  2. Fill up skillet with grapeseed oil till ALMOST able to cover chicken
  3. Turn on skillet to begin heating up
  4. In seperate bowls, mix chicken with egg, flour, milk and bread crumbs
  5. Once the oil is hot enough that when you put a pinch of breadcrumbs in they sizzle the oil is ready
  6. Flash fry the chicken in 5 minutes, chicken should be cooked when done
  7. Optional: If you feel chicken is not thoroughly cooked, you may bake in oven at 400 until suitable (For this step I like topping the chicken with tomato sauce)
  8. Cook angel hair to al dente (should take around 7 minutes)
  9. Head alfredo sauce
  10. Serve and enjoy!
